What is our goal?

Our product is a solution offered to plublic transportation organisations to increase efficiency, reliability and costumer satisfaction. Our product will increase the efficiency of an organisations current transit system by adding self driving busses, adding our app to the transit network and integrating our features into the network to keep riders informed and safe.

Turbo Transpo is a new company seeking to find its place in the city of Ottawa, Canada. Turbo Transpo is a new alternative to the current system OC transpo. We know the struggles the citizens incounter and face everyday. Our company strives for customer trust and reliablity.
